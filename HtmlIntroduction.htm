Cameron's Pig Pen Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken Breast(s)
Parmesan Cheese
Bacon
Teriyaki Sauce
Dijon Mustard

Materials

For this dish, you are going to need a baking dish, prongs (or whatever you use to flip bacon), a pan or skillet to flip the bacon, a stovetop, an oven, and some plates and silverware for when you're finished cooking!

Instructions

To start, cook your bacon as crispy as you like it (note: you will also be baking this bacon in the oven, so it is probably best to undercook the bacon a little bit so you don't burn it too bad). Then you are going to take your chicken breasts and lather them in a nice thin coat of dijon mustard. Then place the chicken in a baking dish, take your cooked bacon and sprinkle it over the top of the chicken, drench the chicken breasts with teriyaki sauce so that the breasts are wading in a small pool of teriyaki sauce(see finished picture for idea), and then sprinkle parmesan cheese all over the top of the chicken. Then bake for 30 minutes on 400 degree F and serve! My finished product is pictured below :)
